Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

CarMate ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the CarMate mobile application.

Legal Compliance: CarMate operates as an intermediary under Section 79 of the Information Technology Act, facilitating private cost-sharing arrangements for fuel and tolls.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a safe and efficient ride-sharing experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identity: Name, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
  • Verification Documents: Government IDs (DL/RC) for driver verification (stored securely and processed manually by admins).
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS coordinates to enable ride matching, live tracking, and SOS features. This is essential for the core functionality of the app.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers for push notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Ride Matching: Connecting drivers and passengers based on location and route.
  • Safety & Security: Facilitating the SOS emergency system and verifying user identities.
  • Communication: Enabling in-app chat between ride participants.
  • Notifications: Sending ride updates, booking confirmations, and safety alerts via OneSignal.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) for secure data storage. All communications between the app and our servers are encrypted using Industry Standard SSL/TLS protocols. Verification documents are stored in private storage buckets accessible only to authorized administrators.

4. Location Permissions

CarMate requires access to your location even when the app is in the background during an active ride to ensure passenger safety and accurate live tracking. You can manage these permissions in your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We share limited data with the following services to provide our core features:

  • OneSignal: For delivering push notifications.
  • Supabase: For backend database and authentication services.
  • OpenStreetMap/OSRM: For mapping and routing (no personal data shared).

6. Data Deletion & Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can request account deletion directly through the app settings or by emailing us. Upon request, all personal data will be permanently removed from our active databases within 30 days.
