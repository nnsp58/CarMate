### Full Description
CarMate is India's premier cost-sharing platform that connects drivers and passengers for smarter, economical travel. Unlike commercial taxi services, CarMate operates as an intermediary under IT Act Section 79, facilitating private arrangements for fuel and toll cost sharing only.

**Key Features:**
🚗 Smart Ride Matching - AI-powered route optimization
📍 Real-time GPS Tracking - Live location monitoring
🆘 Emergency SOS System - 2-second activation with admin alerts
💬 Document Verification - KYC compliance with DL/RC verification
👥 Admin Panel - Complete user management and oversight
💰 Cost Sharing Only - Legal compliance with transparent pricing
🔒 Data Privacy - Encrypted storage with law enforcement access
📱 Instant Chat - Real-time driver-passenger communication

**Benefits:**
